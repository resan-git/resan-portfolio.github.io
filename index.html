Nazmus Sadat

Biomedical Engineering undergraduate with a strong interest in medical image analysis, generative modeling, and fairness in healthcare AI. My work focuses on addressing skin tone bias in dermatological datasets using GAN-based approaches.

Research Interests

  • Generative Adversarial Networks (Pix2Pix, CycleGAN)
  • Medical image synthesis and augmentation
  • Bias and fairness in AI-driven healthcare systems
  • Skin tone representation in dermatology datasets
  • Emerging interest in Large Language Models (LLMs) and RAG systems

Current Focus

My undergraduate thesis investigates controllable generation of skin lesion images across different Fitzpatrick skin tones, aiming to improve dataset diversity and reduce performance disparities in dermatological AI systems.
